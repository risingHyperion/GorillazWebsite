Gorillaz s-a născut în 1998, la inițiativa lui Damon Albarn, solistul trupei Blur, și a artistului grafic Jamie Hewlett, ca un proiect unic care să unească rockul alternativ, muzica pop, hip-hop-ul și muzica electronică.

Gorillaz este o trupă virtuală, iar membrii săi sunt personaje animate, al căror univers este explorat prin intermediul muzicii, videoclipurilor, interviurilor și animațiilor de scurt metraj. Albume

Gorillaz

Gorillaz is the eponymous debut studio album by virtual band Gorillaz. It was released on 26 March 2001 in the United Kingdom by Parlophone and in the United States by Virgin Records. The album reached number three in the UK and number fourteen in the US, and has sold over seven million copies worldwide. The album spawned the singles "Clint Eastwood", "19-2000", "Rock the House", and "Tomorrow Comes Today".

Daemon Days

Demon Days is the second studio album by English virtual band Gorillaz. It was first released on 11 May 2005 in Japan and on 23 May 2005 in the United Kingdom. the album features contributions from De La Soul, Neneh Cherry, Martina Topley-Bird, Roots Manuva, MF DOOM, Ike Turner, Bootie Brown of the Pharcyde, Shaun Ryder, and Dennis Hopper.

Plastic beach

Plastic Beach is the third studio album by British virtual band Gorillaz. It was released on 3 March 2010 by Parlophone internationally and by Virgin Records in the United States and was produced primarily by group co-creator Damon Albarn. Plastic Beach received mostly positive reviews, and was named one of the year's best albums by several critics.
